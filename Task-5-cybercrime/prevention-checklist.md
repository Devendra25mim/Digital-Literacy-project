# 🛡️ Stay Safe Online Checklist

1. Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or bank details with anyone.  
2. Do not scan unknown QR codes or click suspicious payment links.  
3. Always verify the identity of the sender before making any payment.  
4. Use official apps like Google Pay, PhonePe, or Paytm only.  
5. Enable SMS and app notifications for all transactions.  
6. Avoid using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions.  
7. Regularly check your bank statements and transaction history.  
8. Immediately report any fraud to your bank and authorities.
