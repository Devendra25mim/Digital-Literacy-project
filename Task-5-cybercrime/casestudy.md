# Title: UPI Payment Fraud

UPI payment fraud is one of the most common cybercrimes in India, especially among students and online users. In this type of fraud, scammers trick individuals into sharing sensitive information or approving fraudulent payment requests.

A typical case involves a student receiving a phone call or message claiming to be from a bank or payment app. The scammer asks the victim to click on a link or scan a QR code, claiming that money will be received. However, instead of receiving money, the victim unknowingly authorizes a payment from their account.

In some cases, fraudsters also send fake payment links through messaging apps or pretend to be sellers or buyers in online marketplaces. Once the victim enters their details or approves the transaction, money is instantly deducted from their bank account.

Students and inexperienced users are often targeted because they may not be fully aware of how digital payments work. The consequences include financial loss, stress, and difficulty recovering the lost amount.

This case highlights the importance of awareness and careful handling of online transactions.
