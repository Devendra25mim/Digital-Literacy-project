## Overview

This repository presents my Digital Literacy Project for the course CSE0001 at VIT Bhopal. The project showcases essential digital competencies required for students in today’s technology-driven world.

It focuses on building awareness of digital tools, developing a professional online presence, understanding communication practices, and ensuring cybersecurity.

The project is organized into five structured modules, each highlighting a key aspect of digital literacy. Relevant artifacts such as screenshots, documents, and reflections are included, along with a comprehensive final report.

## Project Modules
# Task 1 – Digital Literacy Infographic

## Creation of an informative infographic illustrating key concepts of digital literacy.

# Task 2 – Student Digital Portfolio

## Development of a pehrsonal digital portfolio to showcase academic and professional achievements.

# Task 3 – Coding & Collaboration Platforms

## Exploration and analysis of platforms like GitHub and other collaborative tools used in software development.

# Task 4 – Professional Communication & Email Etiquette

## Understanding formal email writing, communication skills, and responsible social media behavior.

# Task 5 – Cybersecurity & Cybercrime Case Study

Study of real-world cybercrime cases along with preventive measures and safe digital practices.
